# Stay Safe Online – UPI & Cyber Safety Checklist

## ✅ DO’s:
- Always verify the sender before accepting any payment request  
- Use strong passwords and enable app lock on UPI apps  
- Check UPI ID carefully before sending money  
- Enable SMS/email alerts for transactions  
- Report suspicious activity immediately  

## ❌ DON’Ts:
- Never share OTP, UPI PIN, or bank details with anyone  
- Do not click on unknown or suspicious links  
- Do not accept random “collect requests”  
- Avoid using public Wi-Fi for financial transactions  
- Don’t trust unknown callers claiming to be bank officials  

## 💰 UPI-Specific Safety Tips:
- Receiving money does NOT require entering UPI PIN  
- Always double-check before approving payment requests  

## 🚨 Report Cybercrime:
- National Cyber Crime Portal: https://cybercrime.gov.in  
- Helpline: 1930 (24x7)
