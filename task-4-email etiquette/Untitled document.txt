Social Media Do’s and Don’ts for Students


DO’s:
- Maintain a professional and respectful tone in your posts  
- Verify information before sharing it online  
- Use privacy settings to control who can see your content  
- Share meaningful, positive, and informative content  
- Think before posting anything on social media  


DON’Ts:
- Do not share personal or sensitive information publicly  
- Avoid posting offensive or inappropriate content  
- Do not engage in cyberbullying or online arguments  
- Avoid oversharing your daily activities  
- Do not accept friend/follow requests from unknown people
